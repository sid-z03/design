Bio

Azeem Mohammad is a Director of Photography working between India and the United Kingdom. His work explores intimacy, memory, and time — often through natural light and grounded realism. Each frame is guided by a commitment to visual honesty, translating story into imagery that breathes.

Approach

I approach cinematography as a craft where emotion, environment, and narrative intersect through the lens. I work closely with directors to find the visual language that serves the story — never imposing style, but discovering it through the material.
