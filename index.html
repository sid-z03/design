Every frame is a decision. Light is not just illumination — it carries memory, time, and truth.

Azeem Mohammad is a cinematographer based in Hyderabad, India. His work explores intimacy, memory, and time — often through natural light and grounded realism.

Each frame is guided by a commitment to visual honesty, translating story into imagery that breathes. He favours restraint over spectacle, texture over polish.

Working between India and the United Kingdom.
